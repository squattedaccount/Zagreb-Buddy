# Demo scenarios

## Demo 1: The tourist

**User:** *Hey! I just arrived in Zagreb, I have about 2 hours before dinner. I love street art and good coffee.*

**Expected:** Agent activates `graffiti` + `coffee_culture` skills; returns 3–4 places as a walking route from the center; map shows pins; conversational tone.

---

## Demo 2: The rainy day

**User:** *It's raining and I'm bored. What should I do?*

**Expected:** Agent considers weather; suggests indoor places (e.g. cozy café + indoor gallery); mentions what to order; tells a story about the place.

---

## Demo 3: The local

**User:** *I've lived in Zagreb for 5 years. Surprise me with something I probably haven't seen.*

**Expected:** Agent picks the most hidden spots (`tourist_level` 1–2), unusual places, stories that even longtime locals might not know.
