# Overview

## What is Buddy?

An AI-powered local companion that helps both **tourists and locals** discover the real Zagreb — hidden gems, local events, street art, secret courtyards, and authentic experiences that never appear on tourist maps or mainstream search results.

**One-liner:** *Like having a local best friend who always knows the perfect spot.*

## The problem

### For tourists

- See the same handful of attractions that every blog and search engine surfaces
- Generic itineraries with no local context, stories, or personality
- No way to know what's actually happening *right now* — events, festivals, pop-ups
- Hard to find places that locals actually go to

### For locals

- Forget to explore their own city — fall into the same routines
- Don't know what's happening nearby this week or tonight
- Hard to discover new hidden spots even after years of living here
- Want to share their favorite places but have no good tool for it
- No app is built *for them* — everything targets tourists

### The core gap

The real knowledge about a city — the best courtyard café, the street art alley that changes every week, the bar where the jazz is live on Thursdays — lives in people's heads. It doesn't make it into search engines. There's no tool that captures this local intelligence and makes it accessible through natural conversation.

## The solution

A chat-based AI agent that:

- Talks like a local friend, not a search engine
- Knows **38 curated hidden gems** with real local stories across 6 domains
- Has specialized skills (street art, coffee culture, architecture, local bars, hidden courtyards, food & markets)
- Considers time of day, weather, mood, and available hours
- Shows results on an interactive map with walking itineraries
- Lets you save plans to Google Calendar and open routes in Google Maps
- Works for **tourists discovering Zagreb** and **locals rediscovering it**
- Ships as a mobile-friendly web app
